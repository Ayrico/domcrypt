Welcome to MsgDrp

Exchange pseudo-anonymous encrypted messages with your contacts

In order to use this system you need the following:

  • Firefox version 4
  • DOMCrypt Firefox extension which adds privacy enhancements to Firefox
  • An addressbook entry stored on this server.

    Click "Create Addressbook Entry" to begin.

    Your addressbook entry contains data about your "handle", server internet domain and cryptographic data used to secure messages sent to you, so that no one else can read them.

  • You need to know the "handle" or username of the contacts you wish to exchange messages with.

    Click "Addressbook Entry Lookup" to search for your contacts.

    When you find a contact's addressbook entry, you will be prompted to save it so you do not have to keep looking it up.
